Service

YMSL

YMSL Wildcat Chapter Website



Four years and over 80 hours were commited to serving my community during high school through Young Men's Service League Wildcat Chapter. I served as vice president two years and received an award for perfect attendence. The different work I did was spread across the following:

  Minnie's Food Pantry - stocking shelves and helping customers out with food, painting a new thrift shop to help raise more money.
  Plano Senior Center - lead Bingo with the senior occupants of the center.
  Heritage Farmstead - cleaned up the property and also helped set it up for events.
  Sci-Tech Discovery Center - cleaned up and also assisted them with birthday parties.
  Richardson Animal Shelter - walked the dogs and helped them clean up the location.
  St Mark's Catholic Church - painted and also cleaned up the church property.
  HERO's - a day of chaperoning summer camp for kids with special needs.
  Big Brother's Big Sisters - painted a shelter and assisted at a retail store.
  Friends of Plano Library - assisted them with a book sale and fundraiser.
