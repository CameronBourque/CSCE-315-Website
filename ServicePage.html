YMSL

YMSL Wildcat Chapter Website

I worked over 80 hours of community service in YMSL (Young Men's Service League) in the Wildcat chapter. I received a perfect attendence award here for 2 years and was Vice President during that time. The different work I did was spread across the following:

  Minnie's Food Pantry - I assisted with food stocking and helping people who showed up to get food. I also helped them paint a new building that they acquired for their charity.
  Plano Senior Center - I came here multiple times to help lead Bingo with the senior citizens.
  Heritage Farmstead - I helped them clean up the property and also helped set it up for events.
  Sci-Tech Discovery Center - I helped them clean up and also assisted them with birthday parties.
  Richardson Animal Shelter - I walked the dogs and helped them clean up the location.
  St Mark's Catholic Church - I helped them paint and also clean up the church property.
  HERO's - I helped chaperone the kids with special needs at a summer camp.
  Big Brother's Big Sisters - I helped them paint a shelter and assisted at a retail store.
  Friends of Plano Library - I assisted them with a book sale and fundraiser.


NSCS

NSCS TAMU Website
