Above was a project designed that used queues to keep up with drawn circles. Whenever too many circles were drawn it would erase the oldest one and draw a new one in front. Whenever it collided with the edges of the screen it changed colors, direction and speed.

Projects worked on in College

Lego Marble Sorting Project

This is a video of our final presentation for our team's marble sorter project freshman year. This was developed in MatLab originally and then translated to LabView. The coding is what I contributed to the most. The design was built out of different materials which had to fit in a $60 budget. The device runs by first sorting all the marbles into slots. The sorting seperated by material, color and size. After, it scans barcodes and dispenses the correct amount of marbles of each type. In this demonstration it reads 3 different barcodes. When there weren't enough marbles then an alarm would sound. We managed to make it to the contest for the Galveston campus where we placed 2nd and were able to attend the demonstration at College Station.
